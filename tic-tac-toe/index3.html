How to play Tic Tac Toe

Choose a game regime. Point and click in a square to place a X or O mark. Make a diagonal, horizontal or vertical line of 3 squares to win a point.

You can choose with what you play and what type of game you want to try. There are 3 modes, 2 versus PC(one it s easy and one it s hard) and one mode player vs player.
